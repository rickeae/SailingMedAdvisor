Vessel & Crew Contextual Data Engineering
Purpose: This tab manages the Contextual Metadata layer. It integrates structured crew profiles, including allergies, chronic histories, and biometric baselines, with the vessel's operational profile. When a consultation is initiated, this "Medical Identity" is injected into the prompt, enabling personalized, person-specific medicine.
Operational Logic
The goal is to move from "Zero-Shot" medical advice to a fully Context-Aware intervention. By providing MedGemma with a rich profile of the patient, we enhance the model's safety profile, ensuring it accounts for individual contraindications and the unique environmental stressors of the current voyage.
