Health Records Intake Notes
When Logging is on the details of Triage and Inquiry chats are saved here under each crew member's name. If Logging is off then the chats are not saved.

When you reactivate a past chat that chat is loaded into the Triage or Inquiry section and you can continue from where you last left-off. This is useful when you want to resubmit a chat for further consideration by the larger model.

When a crew member has not been selected for a chat the chat will be logged under Unnamed Crew as either Triage chat or an Inquiry chat.
