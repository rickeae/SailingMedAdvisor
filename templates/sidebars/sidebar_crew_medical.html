Consultation Log Clinical Continuity & Auditability
Purpose: The Log serves as the persistent state-management layer of the HAI-DEF architecture. In a disconnected environment, maintaining a local, longitudinal record is critical for clinical continuity. This tab indexes every interaction, allowing the Agent to maintain context over multi-day scenarios where patient status and diagnostic data evolve.
Operational Logic
Beyond a simple transcript, this is a Data Engineering tool. It allows for "Session Restoration," effectively reloading the model's KV cache with previous case history. This ensures that follow-up care is informed by prior observations, creating a verifiable audit trail for shore-side handover and post-incident analysis.
