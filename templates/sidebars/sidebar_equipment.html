Medical Chest
The Medical Chest is our collection of pharmacueticals, medical equipment and medical consumables.

The purpose of the inventory functionality is to inform MedGemma as to the resources available when planning treatments. No point in having it advise treatment with snakebit serum when we have none onboard.

Any medical treatment administered is necesarily constrained by the resources onboard.

There are odd items listed, for example vise grips and needle nose pliers, but they have been needed in the past to remove fish hooks.
